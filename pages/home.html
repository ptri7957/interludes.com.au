About Us

Discover some of the South Coast’s finest self-contained accommodation units at Interludes Cottages at Bawely Point. Set amongst 26 acres of beautiful spotted gum forest and natural bush land, you can choose from one of five architecturally designed one or two bedroom cottages, or our two bedroom hill rise cottage that offers panoramic views to the ocean and Brush Island.

All of our cottages feature contemporary furnishings and have been thoughtfully appointed with your ultimate comfort and convenience foremost in mind. With spacious lounge/dining areas, full sized kitchens, modern bathrooms, two of which contain generous corner spas, and comfortable bedrooms, each cottage offers everything that you need to make the most of your time away. There is nothing for you to do on arrival except put down your bags and make yourself at home.

Situated right in the heart of Bawley, we are merely minutes from the local shops and only a short drive to a number of Bawley’s most stunning beaches. Whether you’re looking for a weekend getaway or an extended holiday haven, Interludes Cottages should be your first choice for luxury accommodation in Bawley Point. Discover more.
