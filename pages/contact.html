Driving Directions

Interludes Cottages is situated approximately 2 hours from Canberra and 3 hours from Sydney.

To get to Bawley Point, turn off the Princes Highway onto Bawley Point Road at Termeil, which is situated between Batemans Bay and Ulladulla. Turn right at Murramarang Road and continue for approximately 1.5 kilometres then turn right onto Forster Drive.

Interludes Cottages is on the left hand side at the top of Forster Drive.
